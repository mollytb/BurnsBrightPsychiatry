Perinatal Mood and Anxiety Disorders (PMAD) are prevalent during pregnancy and postpartum. The resources listed below are for families in Jacksonville to learn about PMADs and find support. UF Health North hosts Mama Birds Support Group: meets 1st and 3rd Friday of each month (flyer attached). Contact name is Beth Higley 904-422-0953. Bay & Bee meets 1x/month at each location - Jacksonville 3rd Friday of every month at 3pm - Bartram Springs 4th Monday at 1pm This is open to the public and membership is not required. https://baynbee.com/pages/calendar-of-events-jax Postpartum Mommies of Northeast Florida FB Group https://www.facebook.com/groups/751128418385621/ Websites with information on PMADs Postpartum Support International https://www.postpartum.net/learn-more/pregnancy-postpartum-mental-health/ www.postpartumprogress.com
