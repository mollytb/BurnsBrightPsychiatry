About Me

Hi! My name is Molly Burns and I’m a full-stack developer. I love doing puzzles and solving mysteries and have found web design and development to be a great way to combine these talents to create new things. Working with others to create something or deconstructing a program to find the bug are both very rewarding and challenging. I’m a full stack developer who would like to work with innovative tech companies to create and adapt the newest technologies to better serve people. I know that technology is one of the best ways to bring people together and to solve some of the world and individual’s most pressing problems.
